Our Services

Supporting Myanmar's tourism industry through regulation, promotion, and development

  1. Home
  2. Services

Hotel Licensing

The Ministry regulates all hotel operations in Myanmar to ensure quality standards and proper classification. We provide:

  • New hotel registration and licensing services
  • Hotel classification and star rating assessments
  • Annual license renewals and inspections
  • Guidance on compliance with tourism regulations

All hotels must be registered with the Ministry before commencing operations. Our team assists with the complete application process and requirements.

Tour Operator Registration

We oversee the registration and regulation of tour companies operating in Myanmar to ensure quality service standards and legal compliance.

  • New tour operator registration and licensing
  • Annual renewals and compliance monitoring
  • Tour guide certification programs
  • Approval for specialized tours (ecotourism, cultural heritage, etc.)

Registered tour operators gain access to official marketing channels and government support programs.

Tourist Visa Information

The Ministry coordinates with immigration authorities to facilitate tourist visa processes and provide up-to-date information:

  • Current visa requirements and application procedures
  • Visa-on-arrival information for eligible countries
  • E-visa application assistance
  • Visa extension services

We work to streamline visa processes while maintaining necessary immigration controls for national security.

Investment Opportunities

Myanmar offers significant investment potential in tourism infrastructure development. Our services include:

  • Guidance on tourism investment policies and regulations
  • Identification of priority investment zones
  • Assistance with investment permits and approvals
  • Information on incentives for tourism projects

We facilitate connections between investors and local partners while ensuring projects align with sustainable tourism goals.

Tourism Statistics

The Ministry collects, analyzes, and publishes comprehensive tourism data to support industry planning and development:

  • Monthly and annual visitor arrival statistics
  • Hotel occupancy and performance data
  • Tourism revenue and expenditure analysis
  • Market research and visitor profile studies

Our statistics department provides reliable data for policymakers, investors, and industry stakeholders.
