Welcome to Myanmar Tourism

The Ministry of Hotel and Tourism is committed to developing Myanmar as a premier travel destination while preserving our rich cultural heritage and natural wonders.

Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism that benefits local communities, protects our environment, and showcases the unique beauty of Myanmar to the world. We work closely with industry partners to enhance visitor experiences while maintaining the authenticity of our traditions.

  • Promoting responsible tourism
  • Preserving cultural heritage
  • Developing tourism infrastructure
  • Supporting local communities
